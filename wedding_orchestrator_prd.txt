OverviewWhat is this product?The AI Wedding Timeline Orchestrator is a smart planning assistant that helps engaged couples in India manage every step of their wedding — from the day they get engaged to the morning of the ceremony. It generates a personalised task timeline, tracks dependencies, and tells the couple exactly what to do next so nothing falls through the cracks.One-line pitch  &quot;A personal wedding planning co-pilot — like having a knowledgeable friend who always knows what needs to happen next.&quot;Who is it for?Engaged couples in India planning their wedding 3–18 months outCouples doing DIY planning or working with a part-time plannerCouples managing multiple events (Mehendi, Sangeet, Haldi, Reception)Families where both sides are involved in task ownershipCore problem it solvesWedding planning in India involves 100+ tasks spread across 6–12 months, multiple events, extended families, and rotating responsibilities. Couples today manage this through WhatsApp groups, spreadsheets, and memory — all of which break down under pressure.Pain PointWhat couples do todayNo single source of truthMultiple WhatsApp groups + Google SheetsDon&apos;t know what to do nextRely on word of mouth / guessworkTask dependencies missedBook invitations before venue is confirmedFamily coordination chaosVerbal assignments, no trackingBudget not connected to tasksSeparate notes, constant recalculationWhy now?Couples in Tier 1 and Tier 2 India are increasingly DIY-planning their weddingsNo product in the market combines AI timelines + multi-event structure + family coordinationWhatsApp is saturated — couples want structured tooling2. AI Logic — Core IntelligenceThe AI does three things in MVP. These are explained in plain English — no technical knowledge required.AI Function 1 — Timeline GenerationWhen the user completes onboarding, the AI takes their inputs and generates a personalised task plan. It works by calculating backwards from the wedding date and placing tasks at the right time based on:How long each task typically takes to completeWhich tasks must come before others (dependencies)The number of events the couple is havingTheir budget range (budget-heavy tasks are surfaced earlier)Their cultural context (adds ceremony-specific tasks)The generation uses a combination of a pre-built Indian wedding task library (200+ tasks) and an LLM call (Claude API) that personalises the selection and ordering based on the specific inputs.AI Function 2 — Dynamic ReprioritisationAs the couple marks tasks done, the AI adjusts the remaining plan:If tasks are delayed, the AI promotes the most time-sensitive upcoming tasksIf the couple is ahead of schedule, the AI surfaces &apos;nice to have&apos; tasks from the libraryEvery time a dependency is resolved, locked tasks automatically unlock and appear in the active listAI Function 3 — Next Best ActionA persistent card on the dashboard shows the couple&apos;s single most important next action. The AI selects this based on:Proximity to deadline (most urgent unblocked task wins)Task weight (booking venue beats ordering favours)Unlocked dependencies (if venue was just booked, invites task moves up)AI implementation note for builders  Timeline generation = one LLM prompt call with wedding details as context.  Reprioritisation = rule-based logic run client-side after each status change.  Next best action = sorted task list, first unblocked item surfaced as a card.  No ML training or fine-tuning required. All intelligence is in the prompt + task library.Sample AI Prompt (for builders)System: You are an expert Indian wedding planner. Generate a detailed week-by-week task plan for an engaged couple based on the inputs below. Output only a structured JSON list of tasks with: task_name, category, event, deadline_weeks_before_wedding, depends_on[], priority (1-5).User inputs: wedding_date, events[], guest_count, budget_range, cultural_context, planning_style.7.
